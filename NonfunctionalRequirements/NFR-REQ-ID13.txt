REQ-ID: 13
Description: Accessing the booking must be a simple process for the user
Type: Usability
Rationale: A difficult process is likely to cause a great deal of user dissatisfaction.
Fit Criterion: Without any prior preparation, 75% of users should be able to access their booking within one minute of the initial contact with the booking system.
